Lawayo is a question and answer site for professional lawyers and law enthusiasts. It's built and run by a community of law experts. With your help, We're working together to build a platform to get detailed answers to every question about Law.

We, At Lawayo, firmly believe that a major part of the successful practice of law is in building sustainable and healthy community. This is the reason we have devised a platform for law experts so that they can harness the power of technology to share, communicate, discuss and spread legal awareness.

We're a little bit different from other sites. Here's how:

Ask Questions, Get Answers, Spread Legal Awareness

Through our dedicated legal advice forum one can directly participate in the ongoing online legal discussion and initiate conversations in the form of questions.

This site is all about getting answers.

Good answers are voted up and rise to the top.

The best answers show up first so that they are always easy to find.

The person who asked can mark one answer as "accepted".

Accepting doesn't mean it's the best answer, it just means that it worked for the person who asked.

Tags make it easy to find relevant questions

All questions are tagged with their subject areas. Each can have up to 5 tags, since a question might be related to several subjects.

Click any tag to see a list of questions with that tag, or go to the tag list to browse for topics that interest you.

You earn reputation when people vote on your posts

Lawayo has Karma:

  • Experts are rewarded with karma for posting useful information
  • Upvotes grow users karma and the downvotes decrease it
  • Votes identify best content, while karma spotlights and rewards experts
  • User privileges grow along with their reputation.
  • Choose either public, private or hidden karma.

Improve posts by editing or commenting

Our goal is to have the best answers to every question, so if you see questions or answers that can be improved, you can edit them.

Use edits to fix mistakes, improve formatting, or clarify the meaning of a post.

Use comments to ask for more information or clarify a question or answer.

You can always comment on your own questions and answers. Once you earn 50 reputation, you can comment on anybody's post.

Remember: we're all here to learn, so be friendly and helpful!

Unlock badges for special achievements

Badges are special achievements you earn for participating on the site. They come in three levels: bronze, silver, and gold.
